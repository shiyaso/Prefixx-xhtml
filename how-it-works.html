Prefixx is a one-stop shop for all your IPv4 transactions

We are extremely familiar with the RIR procedures. In fact, we helped write the current RIPE transfer policies, and we adhere to them by the letter.

Upon receiving a request or quote, we check that the provider is authorized to trade, and then contact the requesting party. As an additional service, we can manage and clean the IP blocks involved.

All payments are handled by Prefix Broker's primary notary. The notary manages the funds through a third-party account until the transaction is completed and the IPv4 addresses are registered to their new owner. After receiving a registration confirmation from the RIR, we instruct the notary to finalize the payment to the selling party.

We work to ensure that all parties involved in a transaction receive full entitlement after completion of the deal. Prefix Broker guides the process start to finish and handles all details in a professional, transparent and proper manner.
